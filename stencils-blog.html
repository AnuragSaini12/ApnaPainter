What Makes Wall Stencils Better Than Wallpapers and Wall Decals?

  1. To begin with, wall stencils are reusable and can be used multiple times unlike wallpapers.
  2. Wall stencils are made of paint so they are durable in comparison to wall decals
  3. Wallpapers and Wall decals are stuck with adhesive to walls and can do more damage compared to Wall Stencils
  4. Wall stencils are cost-effective and for the long run
  5. Wall stencils are moisture-proof whereas wallpapers and decals are not

Trending Wall Stencils for Living Room Walls

In comparison to expensive tiling, wallpapers and wall decals, wall stencils for living rooms walls are a great, cost-effective idea that can transform your space. Our experts have rounded up unique stencil designs for living rooms that can inspire you.

Cherry Blossom Wall Stencils for Living Room Walls :

The lively and enriching cherry blossom visuals can be a perfect backdrop to your living room where gatherings are held. Preferred in white colour, beige, cream, pink or lavender, cherry blossom wall stencil design for living rooms is a pleasant and creative way to create an inviting living area.

Wall Stencil Designs for Living Rooms Incorporating Messages :

For the readers, the dreamers alike, let your favourite quote or saying be the one that keeps you company. All you have to do is choose the typography, the quote and customise a stencil for it to be painted on your wall. Not only will it leave you inspired, but guests will also appreciate the thoughtfulness over a bare, ordinary wall.

Creative Scallops Wall Stencil Design for Living Room :

Let scallops enrich a vacant wall in your living room to add dimension and character to your space. An idle wall may not be as aesthetically pleasing as a statement wall with a royal blue scallops wall stencil design for the living room. Scallop stencils range from intricate traditional designs to modern, minimal ones. You can choose your own as per your interiors!

A Simple Geometry Trick as Wall Stencil Designs :

If you’re wondering what to do with your bare wall, we have a solution. This trending geometry wall stencil design can add dimension and character to your wall. Just use a wall stencil to draw random lines across your wall, making it a larger wall art design. This not only creates a masterpiece in your living room, but also adds colour to your space.

Popular Tribal Feathers as Wall Stencil in Living Rooms :

Looking for a bohemian, tribal accent in your living room? This trend is what you need to go after. Intricate tribal feather wall stencils can be painted across a single wall in your living room to add a rustic and earthy touch. You can either use wall paint of the same family or create a contrasting visual with this design. And that is how you can have a boho-chic living room.

Moroccan Tile Designs Used As Wall Stencils in Living Rooms :

Don’t worry if you have never stepped foot in Morocco! Let’s bring the middle eastern royal touch to your living room walls. Use wall stencils that recreate Marhaba or Ikat-inspired Moroccan tile designs as wall stencils in your living room, to create a rich and sophisticated space. This is also a cost-effective way to save yourself from the expenses of tiling or panelling.
