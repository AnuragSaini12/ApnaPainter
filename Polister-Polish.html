Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question or a doubt? We've got the answers right here

1. What is Polister Polish, and how does it benefit my home?

Polister Polish is a high-quality coating designed to enhance and protect various surfaces in your home, such as walls, exteriors, and furniture. It offers several benefits, including enhanced aesthetics, increased durability, and resistance to moisture, UV rays, and other environmental factors. With Polister Polish, you can achieve a smooth, luxurious finish that adds value and longevity to your property.

2. Is Polister Polish suitable for both interior and exterior surfaces?

Yes, Polister Polish is versatile and can be applied to both interior and exterior surfaces. Whether you want to refresh the walls in your living room or protect the siding of your home, our Polister Polish services are tailored to meet your specific needs. Our expert technicians will ensure that the appropriate Polister Polish formulation is used for each surface, providing optimal results for every application.

3. How long does Polister Polish last, and is it easy to maintain?

The longevity of Polister Polish depends on various factors, including the type of surface, environmental conditions, and maintenance practices. However, on average, Polister Polish can last several years with proper care and maintenance. To prolong its lifespan, we recommend regular cleaning with mild soap and water and avoiding harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners. Additionally, periodic touch-ups or reapplications may be necessary to maintain the integrity of the finish over time.

4. Can Polister Polish be applied to all types of furniture?

Yes, Polister Polish can be applied to a wide range of furniture materials, including wood, metal, and laminate. Whether you have antique pieces that need restoration or modern furnishings that could use a protective finish, our Polister Polish services are customizable to suit your needs. Our expert craftsmen will assess the condition of your furniture and recommend the most appropriate Polister Polish treatment to enhance its beauty and durability.

5. Is Polister Polish environmentally friendly and safe for my family and pets?

Yes, our Polister Polish products are formulated to meet stringent environmental and safety standards. They are low in volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and free from harmful chemicals, making them safe for use in residential settings. Our team takes every precaution to ensure that your family and pets are not exposed to any health risks during the Polister Polish application process. Additionally, once cured, Polister Polish creates a protective barrier that is non-toxic and safe for everyday use.
