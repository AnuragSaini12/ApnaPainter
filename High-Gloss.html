Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question or a doubt? We've got the answers right here

1. What is PU High Gloss Polish, and how does it differ from other types of finishes?

PU High Gloss Polish is a premium finishing technique that involves applying polyurethane coatings to surfaces to achieve a glossy, mirror-like sheen. Unlike other finishes, such as matte or satin, PU High Gloss Polish offers a high level of reflectivity and durability, making it ideal for enhancing the aesthetic appeal and longevity of surfaces.

2. What surfaces can be treated with PU High Gloss Polish?

Our PU High Gloss Polish services are versatile and can be applied to a wide range of surfaces, including walls, ceilings, wooden furniture, fixtures, and more. Whether you're looking to revitalize your interior spaces or add a luxurious finish to specific elements in your home or office, PU High Gloss Polish can help you achieve stunning results.

3. How long does the PU High Gloss Polish process take, and how soon can I use the treated surfaces?

The duration of the PU High Gloss Polish process depends on factors such as the size of the project and the condition of the surfaces. Typically, our skilled professionals complete the process efficiently, ensuring minimal disruption to your daily routine. As for when you can use the treated surfaces, they are usually ready for light use within 24 to 48 hours after application, but full curing may take up to several days.

4. Is PU High Gloss Polish environmentally friendly and safe for indoor use?

Yes, our PU High Gloss Polish formulations are carefully selected to prioritize environmental sustainability and indoor air quality. We use low-VOC (volatile organic compound) formulations that minimize emissions and odors, making them safe for indoor use. Additionally, our eco-friendly approach ensures that you can enjoy the beauty of your space without compromising on environmental responsibility.

5. How do I maintain surfaces treated with PU High Gloss Polish to ensure their longevity?

Maintaining surfaces treated with PU High Gloss Polish is simple and straightforward. We recommend regular dusting and cleaning with a mild detergent solution and a soft cloth to remove dirt and debris. Avoid abrasive cleaners and harsh chemicals, as they can dull the finish over time. With proper care and maintenance, your PU High Gloss Polish surfaces will retain their beauty and shine for years to come.
